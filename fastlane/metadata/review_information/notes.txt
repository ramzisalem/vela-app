Vela is a face-tracking wellness app for measurement and routine guidance. It is NOT a medical device and makes no medical claims. The app shows progress on user-recorded photos with weekly comparisons.

REQUIREMENTS FOR REVIEW
- Vela requires the TrueDepth camera (iPhone X or later). The Apple review device must support this.
- A 7-day free trial is offered for both monthly ($9.99) and annual ($79) subscriptions.

DEMO ACCOUNT
Vela does not require demo credentials — anyone can create an account with Sign in with Apple or email. A "Skip onboarding (TestFlight)" option exists in the development build to help reviewers reach the dashboard quickly. The TestFlight build behaves identically to production except for the skip option.

PERMISSIONS
- Camera (NSCameraUsageDescription): required for face tracking. Permission is requested only when the user begins their first scan.
- App Tracking Transparency (NSUserTrackingUsageDescription): requested AFTER the baseline-reveal screen and BEFORE the paywall (per file 31). Singular MMP attribution only initializes after consent (granted or denied).
- Photo library (NSPhotoLibraryAddUsageDescription): requested only when the user taps "Save to Photos" on a share card. The app never saves to Photos automatically.
- Notifications: requested after the first scan completes. All notification surfaces respect a per-week budget (file 12).

DATA HANDLING
- All photos remain on-device. We never transmit raw photos, depth maps, or face landmarks to our servers.
- The AI proxy receives only numeric metrics derived on-device.
- HealthKit (when granted) is read-only for cycle, sleep, and activity correlations and is fully optional.
- Auth tokens stored in SecureStorage / Keychain only.

CANCEL FLOW
Subscription cancel is a single tap from Settings → Subscription. The save flow is a single bottom sheet with one offer; declining proceeds to cancellation. There are no multi-step "are you sure?" friction screens.
